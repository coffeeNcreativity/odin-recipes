Arroz Amarillo

Simple and full of tast, plus beautifully colored by safron, this rellow rice is an everyday staple in many hispanic homes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 packets of Goya's Saz√≥n con Azafran
  • 2 tspn of salt
  • 1/4 cup of cooking oil
  • (Optional)1 cup chicken stock
  1. Add the water,(Optional chicken stock.),salt, and oil to a caldero, or other large pot, and then bring them to a boil.
  2. Rinse, then add rice to the caldero.
  3. Boil rice until the water is mostly gone, to the point where you can only see bubbles of water coming up through the grains of rice. AVOID STIRRING THE RICE, ony using a wooden spoon to lift and turn the rice over as needed to avoid it sticking to the bottom of the caldero.
  4. Once the liquid has boilded away, cover the caldero/pot with a lid, turn the temperature from high to low, and cook for 30 more minutes.
  5. Let the rice sit for 10 minutes with the heat off, then enjoy!
