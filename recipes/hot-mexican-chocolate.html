Mexican Hot Chocolate

Hot, sweet, and the right amount of comfort on on a dreary day! Or when you are sick, or just because! This Mexican favorite will leave you feeling like you just got a hug from the world's best aubelita!

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon of whole milk
  • 3 Aubelita's brand hot chocolate tablets(Or your preference)
  • 3 cups sugar
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • Add extra milk as needed
  1. Add milk to a large pot and heat until boiling, while constantly stirring.
  2. Add sugar, cinnamon sticks, and Abuelita's chocolate to the boiling milk. Don't forget; DO NOT stop stirring!
  3. Boild the ingredients untill all of the chocolate disks have disolved.
  4. Finally, turn of the heat and enjoy hot! Careful not to burn yourself!
