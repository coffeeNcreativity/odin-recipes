Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Simple, efficient, and delicious! There may not be a lot that goes into this dish, bu that doesn't stop it from being a major go to for many families.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread of your choice. We use whole wheat!
  • Your preferred flavor of jelly. We prefer strawberry in our household!
  • Your favorite brand and type of peanut butter.
  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread.
  2. Spread Jelly on the other slice of bread.
  3. Put both slices of bread together with the sides of the bread containing the peanut butter and jelly making contact.
  4. Enjoy!
